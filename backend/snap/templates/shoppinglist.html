Find new recipes based on your shopping list

Instructions

Enter your shopping list, one item at a time. We will try to generate some recipes you can make with just those ingredients, or possibly a few more.

These recipes are intended to be quick, inexpensive, and easy to prepare. Good food doesn't need expensive ingredients and hours of labor. Exploring new recipes can bring new life to your food, even if you have the same ingredients every week.
