Welcome to SNAPpy


Access to good food shouldn't be hard.


Welcome to SNAPpy - the one-stop solution for individuals in food deserts who rely on the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP). This innovative website/web-service is designed to provide all the necessary information and resources needed to make grocery shopping a breeze.

Our grocery retailer locator is a game-changer for SNAP beneficiaries. It not only helps you find food stamp accepted stores near you but also provides crucial information about their accessibility and handicap facilities. No more struggling to navigate unfamiliar neighborhoods and stores.

Moreover, SNAPpy's grocery list feature is a real lifesaver. Simply input your shopping list, and our app will come up with delicious and healthy recipes that can be made with the ingredients you already have. No more worrying about what to cook or eating unhealthy meals because of limited resources.

At SNAPpy, our mission is to support and empower individuals in food deserts. We understand the challenges faced by these communities and are committed to providing them with the tools and resources they need to live healthy and fulfilling lives. Join us today and take control of your food choices and well-being!
